Message from Our Chairman

There are few responsibilities as significant to parents as the education of their children. Every parent aspires for academic excellence, strong values, and disciplined growth for their child. Therefore, the selection of the right educational institution is an immensely critical choice.

At NAFL Jakkur, we uphold elevated standards for educational brilliance for our students. We foster innovation and cultivate problem-solving skills within each student. Guided by a family-centric approach, we firmly believe that the collaboration between the family and the school forms the bedrock of a student's contentment and accomplishments in their future endeavors.

In today's rapidly evolving world, leading educational institutions strive to introduce and embrace a creative approach within the curriculum. Educators play a pivotal role in implementing and bolstering students to achieve excellence in their pursuits.

Our mission at NAFL Jakkur is to establish a joyful, harmonious, and stimulating milieu where our students can nurture their unique talents and aspirations. Simultaneously, we offer an array of cultural, athletic, and co-curricular engagements, fostering leadership in a prudent and respectful setting.

Registration Process at NAFL Jakkur School

  • Kindly utilize the provided links located at the conclusion of this document to access the Registration Forms.
  • Thoroughly fill out the form online, attach the requested documents, and make a payment of Rs. 1250/- (non-refundable) via the online payment gateway.
  • Afterwards, download and print the completed Registration Form along with the Registration Fee Receipt.
  • Ensure the submission of a hard copy of the Registration Form, the Registration Fee Receipt, and copies of the requested documents at the school office. Please be informed that this step is obligatory. Solely submitting the form online will not suffice.
  • For families residing outside India, seeking admission to NAFL Jakkur, please send us an email at admin@nafljakkur.in once the registration process is concluded. We will get in touch with you through email as soon as a seat becomes available.
  • Our school office will be accessible from Monday to Friday, spanning 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.

Admission Process at NAFL Jakkur

NAFL Jakkur School maintains a limited number of available seats. Please note that the submission of the Registration Form does NOT guarantee admission. Any attempt to exert influence directly or indirectly for securing admission will result in disqualification. The priority evaluation of applications is conducted under the following circumstances:

  • Students applying from outside India
  • Students seeking admission to grades 9 and 11
  • Siblings of current NAFL Jakkur students (Not Applicable for us since its a new school)

Admission is strictly based on merit and the availability of seats.

Upon successful clearance of the Entrance Test, parents and students will have the opportunity to engage with the Principal. Furthermore, an orientation session regarding the academic curriculum, school policies, and routines will be conducted in May-June, preceding the commencement of the academic year.
